Acne affects people of all ages, and laser technology offers a powerful tool in the treatment arsenal — especially for stubborn breakouts and scarring.

## How Lasers Help Acne

Different laser wavelengths target distinct aspects of acne: reducing P. acnes bacteria, decreasing sebum production, calming inflammation, and stimulating collagen to improve scarring.

## IPL vs. Other Modalities

Intense pulsed light (IPL) is effective for inflammatory acne and post-inflammatory redness. More aggressive lasers may address deeper scarring. Your provider will match technology to your skin type and concerns.

## What to Expect

Most protocols involve a series of treatments spaced weeks apart. Sun protection and proper aftercare are essential for optimal results and safety.

Schedule a skin consultation to determine the best laser approach for your acne concerns.
