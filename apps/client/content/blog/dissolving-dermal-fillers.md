Not every filler result goes exactly as planned — whether due to migration, overfilling, or simply a change in preference. The good news: hyaluronic acid fillers can be safely dissolved.

## What Is Filler Dissolver?

Hyaluronidase is an enzyme that breaks down hyaluronic acid fillers. When injected precisely, it dissolves unwanted filler within 24–48 hours, allowing correction or a fresh start.

## When Is Dissolving the Right Choice?

Common reasons include asymmetry, lumps, overfilling, or wanting to try a different approach. Dissolving isn't a failure — it's a tool that gives you and your provider flexibility.
