Understanding where Botox is injected helps demystify the process and set realistic expectations for your results.

## Common Treatment Areas

**Forehead lines** — horizontal lines from raising eyebrows. **Glabellar lines** — the "11s" between brows from frowning. **Crow's feet** — lines at the outer corners of eyes from squinting. **Brow lift** — strategic placement can subtly elevate the brow arch.

## Beyond the Obvious

Botox can also address a gummy smile, chin dimpling, lip flip, and masseter slimming. Each area requires specific technique and dosing.

## The Art of Placement

Injection site selection isn't one-size-fits-all. Your provider considers your muscle strength, facial symmetry, and aesthetic goals to create a customized map for each treatment.
