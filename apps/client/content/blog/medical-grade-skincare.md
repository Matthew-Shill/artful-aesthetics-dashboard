The skincare aisle can be overwhelming. Medical-grade products offer something drugstore brands often can't: higher concentrations of active ingredients backed by clinical research.

## What Makes Skincare "Medical Grade"

Medical-grade products contain pharmaceutical-level active ingredients (retinoids, growth factors, peptides) at concentrations that require professional guidance. They're dispensed through licensed providers who can customize regimens.

## Why It Matters

Effective skincare prevents and complements in-office treatments. The right regimen can extend neurotoxin and filler results, improve texture, and address pigmentation between visits.

## Personalized Is Key

There's no universal "best" product — your skin type, concerns, and treatment history determine what works. A provider-guided approach eliminates guesswork and wasted spending.
